# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## Introduction

Wardrobe Assistance ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Clothing Items**: Photos and metadata (category, color, season, style) that you add to your wardrobe
- **Outfits**: Collections of clothing items you create
- **Preferences**: Style preferences and settings you configure

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (features used, frequency of use)
- **Device Information**: Device type, iOS version, app version
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

## How We Use Your Information

- **Core Functionality**: To provide wardrobe management, outfit recommendations, and style analytics
- **AI Features**: To generate personalized outfit recommendations based on your wardrobe
- **App Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience
- **Customer Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All clothing photos and wardrobe data are stored **locally on your device**
- Images are saved in the app's document directory
- No data is transmitted to our servers without your explicit consent

### iCloud Sync (Premium Feature)
- **Optional**: iCloud sync is available as a Premium feature
- If enabled, your data is synced to your personal iCloud account
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We do not have access to your iCloud data

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your personal data with third parties.**

- No data is sold to advertisers or data brokers
- No analytics services are used that collect personal information
- No third-party services have access to your wardrobe data

## Subscription Information

- Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
- We do not store payment information
- Subscription status is verified through StoreKit 2
- Subscription data is managed by Apple

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You can view all your data within the app
- **Delete**: You can delete individual items or clear all data
- **Export**: You can export your wardrobe data (Premium feature)
- **Unsubscribe**: Cancel your subscription anytime through App Store settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
